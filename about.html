ME

I'm a Computer Science / Music Major from Northeastern.

The Art Journey

After stumbling upon random digital art videos on Youtube in May 2021, I was inspired to start myself. I was always a fan of Japanese animation, and it seemed like a fun hobby to pick up. The main way I improved was by watching a lot of Youtube videos, analyzing good art, and practicing deliberately.

Inktober 2021

In order to get better at art, I did a thing called Inktober, where I drew one drawing every day for each day in October. I started with proportion practice. The order is from left to right, top to down. I copy drew many drawings I liked afterwards to get a feel for good art. In the second half, I practiced coloring, and in the end, I spent a bit more time to finish something without a reference.
